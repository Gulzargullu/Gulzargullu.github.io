About Me

From operations to analytics — the long way around.

I didn't start my career staring at dashboards. I started on the floor — 2 years in customer service where I learned one thing fast: the problems people complain about are almost always connected to data nobody's looking at.

That curiosity pushed me into IT and MIS roles, where I spent 2 more years working with business data, reporting systems and operational metrics. I hold a BCA degree and have been building on that foundation ever since.

I'm passionate about visual storytelling with data. I believe a good chart doesn't just show numbers — it makes someone go "oh, so that's what's happening."
